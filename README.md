## Car Jokes

Here are some jokes about cars:

1. **Why did the car apply for a job?** Because it wanted to get a little more mileage out of life!

2. **What kind of car does a sheep drive?** A Lamborghini!

3. **Why don't cars ever get lonely?** Because they're always getting driven around!

4. **What's a car's favorite meal?** A traffic jam!

5. **Why did the car break up with the road?** It was tired of being taken for granted!
